# Chetana Distribution OS

## Core loop

watch → check → share → protect

## Channel roles

- Chetana
  Conversion and protection surface.
- MirrorProd
  Content generation engine.
- Instagram / Facebook
  Fastest demand-testing lane.
- WhatsApp
  Share loop and family protection loop.
- Google / Search
  Intent capture.
- YouTube Shorts
  Repeatable scale layer.
- LinkedIn
  Credibility and partnership layer.

## Product implication

Distribution is not a sidecar.
Every Chetana entry point should preserve attribution and route context:
- entry source
- creative or topic
- language
- platform
- whether the user checked, completed, and shared

## Weekly operating rhythm

- 3 scam lanes
- 9 short videos
- 3 language variants
- 1 LinkedIn operating note
- 1 search article
- 1 WhatsApp share card

## What the product must expose

- deep links into message / QR / emergency mode
- source tagging in every app-open event
- share-safe warning copy per scam lane
- family-forwardable warning card
- conversion analytics from view to check to share

## Why this matters

The content engine creates demand.
The product must turn that demand into:
- completed checks
- saved payments
- saved installs
- saved family members
