# Emergency Playbooks

Every emergency lane must provide:
- first five minutes
- official rails
- evidence to preserve
- what not to do
- a ready-to-speak handoff line

## Already installed APK or gave remote access

First five minutes:
1. Turn on airplane mode or disconnect from the network.
2. Do not open banking, UPI, wallet, or loan apps on the suspected phone.
3. Use another clean device to call your bank or payment provider.
4. Revoke accessibility, device-admin, notification, and screen-share access if present.
5. Call `1930`, then file or complete the NCRP complaint.

Official rails:
- `1930`
- NCRP portal
- RBI CMS if the bank or PSP response is inadequate

## Paid through QR or active money loss

First five minutes:
1. Capture the transaction ID, payee VPA, amount, and screenshot.
2. Call your bank or payment provider immediately.
3. Block UPI, cards, linked wallet rails, and netbanking if needed.
4. Call `1930` fast while the freeze window is still open.
5. Check the suspect identifier in the I4C suspect repository.

## Shared OTP / PIN / password

First five minutes:
1. Stop using the compromised session.
2. Call the bank or payment provider from another device.
3. Reset PINs, passwords, and recovery settings from a clean device.
4. Watch for mandates, loan creation, and beneficiary changes.
5. Report to `1930` and NCRP.

## Digital arrest / coercive authority scam

First five minutes:
1. End the call or video session.
2. Do not move money to any “safe account”.
3. Call a trusted family member on a known number.
4. If there is active physical threat or coercion, use `112`.
5. File cyber-fraud reporting through `1930` and NCRP.

## Suspicious overseas job / travel

First five minutes:
1. Do not board and do not surrender your passport.
2. Call family or a trusted contact immediately.
3. Verify the recruiter and visa path through eMigrate / MEA guidance.
4. Preserve recruiter chats, tickets, receipts, passport copies, and offer letters.
5. If already abroad or under coercion, contact local police and the nearest Indian mission.

## Universal evidence checklist

- screenshots
- phone numbers
- URLs
- APK file or filename
- transaction IDs / UTR
- payee VPA or account
- timestamps
- recruiter / employer / ticket documents
