2. The Short Version

Chetana scans and forgets.
  • No accounts. No login. No sign-up.
  • Your scans are checked and not stored.
  • Your data is processed by local AI models on our servers — nothing is sent to external AI companies (no OpenAI, no Google AI, no cloud AI APIs).
  • We collect zero personal information.
  • We track only anonymous scan counts (e.g., "400 scans today") for service health.

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 During a Scan (Not Stored)

When you submit a message, link, UPI ID, or phone number for checking:

  • Your submission is processed in real time by our local AI models (Ollama) and pattern-matching engine
  • A risk result is generated and shown to you
  • The submitted content and the result are not saved after the response is sent
  • No logs of individual scans are kept

3.2 Anonymous Analytics (Aggregated Only)

What We Track Example Contains PII?
Total scan count "1,247 scans today" No
Scan type breakdown "60% messages, 25% links, 15% UPI" No
Language usage "40% Hindi, 30% English" No
Risk level distribution "22% flagged as high risk" No

These are aggregate counters only. They cannot be traced back to any individual user or scan.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:
  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers of users
  • Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, or any government ID
  • Bank account numbers, credit card details, or financial data
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, IMEI, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • IP addresses (not logged)
  • Contacts, call logs, SMS history, or browsing history
  • Photos, videos, or biometric data
  • User accounts, profiles, or login credentials (there are none)

5. No Aadhaar Data

Chetana does not accept Aadhaar data. Do not submit your Aadhaar number. If an Aadhaar number appears in text you submit for scanning, it is not extracted, stored, or processed separately.

6. How Your Data Is Processed

When you submit something for scanning:

  1. Your text reaches our server over an encrypted connection (HTTPS)
  2. Our local AI models (Ollama, running on our own infrastructure) and pattern-matching rules analyze it
  3. A risk score and explanation are generated
  4. The result is sent back to you
  5. The submitted text and result are discarded — not written to any database or log file

No data is sent to external AI APIs. No OpenAI, no Google AI, no Anthropic, no cloud-based LLM services. All AI processing happens on our own servers.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The Chetana website:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not use tracking pixels
  • May use localStorage in your browser to remember your language preference. This data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

10. Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

10.1 Right to Access (Section 11)

You can ask us what data we hold about you. Since we do not store scan data or personal information, the answer in most cases will be: nothing.

To make a request, email paul@activemirror.ai.

10.2 Right to Erasure (Section 13)

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you. Since scan data is not stored, there is generally nothing to delete. If you have used the Telegram bot and want your Telegram-side conversation removed, you can delete the chat on Telegram directly.

10.3 Right to Withdraw Consent

You consent to processing by submitting a scan. You can withdraw at any time by simply not using the service. No data persists after you stop.

10.4 Right to Correction (Section 12)

If any data we hold about you is incorrect, contact us at paul@activemirror.ai and we will correct it.

10.5 Right to Grievance Redressal (Section 14)

If you have a complaint about how we handle your data:

  1. Email paul@activemirror.ai
  2. We will respond within 30 days
  3. If you are not satisfied, you may escalate through any channel available under Indian law

10.6 Right to Nominate (Section 14(2))

You may nominate another person to exercise your data rights in case of death or incapacity. Contact us to register a nominee.

11. Children's Data (DPDP Act Section 9)

Chetana is intended for users aged 18 and above.

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18
  • We do not have age verification — parental supervision is advised
  • We do not track, profile, or target any user
  • If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us for immediate deletion

12. Data Security (IT Act Section 43A)

  • All connections to Chetana use HTTPS encryption
  • AI processing happens on our own infrastructure in India
  • No user data is stored in cloud databases
  • No remote access to any stored data

13. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Chetana processes all scan data on servers in India. Cloudflare (CDN) may route your web request through servers outside India as part of standard internet delivery, but no personal data is stored by Cloudflare on our behalf.

14. Data Breach Notification

If a data breach ever occurs:

  1. We will contain it immediately
  2. We will report to CERT-In within 6 hours (per CERT-In Directions 2022)
  3. We will notify affected users without unreasonable delay
  4. We will document and publish what happened and what we did to fix it

Since we do not store personal data from scans, the risk surface is minimal.

15. Automated Decision-Making

Chetana uses automated AI models and rules to generate risk scores. These scores:

  • Are informational only — they do not trigger any automatic action
  • Do not result in any legal or financial consequence to anyone
  • Are not accusations of fraud against any person or business
  • Can be reviewed if you believe an assessment is incorrect (email us)

16. Purpose Limitation (DPDP Act Section 6)

Any data processed by Chetana is used only for scam detection. We will never use it for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • User profiling or behavioral targeting
  • Sale or sharing with third parties
  • Training AI models on your personal data
  • Government surveillance (unless compelled by a valid Indian court order)
